`(yas-with-comment "BUG: ")`

** dd: Debug
`(yas-with-comment "DEBUG: ")`

** ff: Fixme
`(yas-with-comment "FIXME: ")`

** hh: Hack
`(yas-with-comment "HACK: ")`

** nn: Note
`(yas-with-comment "NOTE: ")`

** rr: Remove
`(yas-with-comment "REMOVE: ")`

** ss: Stub
