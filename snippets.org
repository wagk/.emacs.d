* org-mode

** #: org-block
#+BEGIN_${1:SRC} $2
$0
#+END_$1

** --: org-list-with-checkbox
- [ ] $0

** src: org-mode-code-block
#+BEGIN_SRC ${1:emacs-lisp}
#+END_SRC

** math: Latex math environment
\begin{equation}
$0
\end{equation}

** elisp: org-mode-elisp-code-block
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp :results output silent
  $0
#+END_SRC
